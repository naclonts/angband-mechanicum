# Character creation flow: class, homeworld, stats, and randomization

Expand CharacterSetupScreen into a multi-step character creation flow. Currently only name selection exists. New flow should include:

## Steps

1. **Name** — existing name selection (keep current suggestions + input)
2. **Class/Specialization** — determines starting stats, abilities, and narrative flavor:
   - **Magos Explorator** — discovery-focused Tech-Priest. Bonus to exploration, archaeotech identification, xenos-tech analysis. Balanced combat.
   - **Magos Dominus** — battlefield commander. Bonus to combat, Skitarii command, weapon mastery. Leads from the front.
   - **Enginseer** — field mechanic and repair specialist. Bonus to crafting, repair, machine-spirit communion. Defensive combat.
   - **Genetor** — flesh-sculptor and biologis researcher. Bonus to biology, mutation resistance, medicae. Unorthodox.
   - **Logis** — data-savant and probability engine. Bonus to analysis, planning, trap detection, information warfare.
   - **Skitarii Alpha** — augmented soldier, not a full Tech-Priest. High combat stats, low tech mastery, squad tactics.
   - **Rogue Astartes** — Space Marine gone walkabout. Massive combat power, minimal tech skill, outsider to AdMech hierarchy. Rare/hard-mode choice.
3. **Homeworld** — narrative flavor + minor stat modifiers:
   - **Mars** — the Red Planet, heart of the Mechanicus. Balanced, orthodox, high starting prestige.
   - **Metalica** — the Furnace of Wrath. Militant forge world. Combat bonus.
   - **Ryza** — plasma specialists. Bonus to energy weapons and plasma tech, risk of overheating.
   - **Stygies VIII** — xenos tech researchers, borderline heretical. Bonus to forbidden knowledge, penalty to prestige.
   - **Agripinaa** — war-scarred bastion near the Eye of Terror. Toughness bonus, corruption resistance.
   - **Lucius** — the Hollow Forge. Master tunnelers and void-adapted. Bonus to dungeon navigation and low-light environments.
   - **Graia** — stubborn and unyielding. Morale/willpower bonus, resist mind-control and panic.
   - **Underhive Gutter** — no forge world at all. Scrappy, resourceful, penalty to prestige but bonus to scavenging and improvisation.
4. **Stats** — core attributes, point-buy or rolled:
   - **Ballistic Skill (BS)** — ranged weapon accuracy
   - **Weapon Skill (WS)** — melee combat effectiveness
   - **Toughness** — damage resistance, physical endurance
   - **Tech Affinity** — machine communion, repair, crafting
   - **Lore** — knowledge of arcane technology, xenos, warp
   - **Willpower** — corruption resistance, mental fortitude
   Each stat starts at a base (e.g. 10) modified by class and homeworld. Player gets bonus points to allocate.
5. **Augmetic Loadout** — starting cybernetic implant (pick 1):
   - **Mechadendrite Array** — extra manipulator limbs, bonus to multi-tasking and repair
   - **Baleful Eye** — ocular laser implant, ranged attack option
   - **Cortex Implant** — cogitator upgrade, bonus to Lore and analysis
   - **Refractor Field** — personal shield generator, damage reduction
   - **Omnispex** — enhanced sensor suite, bonus to detection and FOV range
   - **Luminen Capacitors** — bioelectric weapon, melee shock attack

## Randomize Option

A prominent ROLL THE DICE / COMMUNE WITH THE OMNISSIAH button at the top of the flow that randomizes all selections (name, class, homeworld, stat allocation, augmetic) and presents the result for the player to accept or re-roll. The player can also randomize individual steps.

## Data Model Changes

- Add CharacterBuild dataclass to hold class, homeworld, stats, augmetic
- Thread CharacterBuild through to GameEngine alongside player_name
- Stats should influence gameplay (combat modifiers, skill checks, FOV, etc.)
- Persist in save files
- Display relevant stats in InfoPanel

## UI

- Multi-step wizard within CharacterSetupScreen (or a new screen sequence)
- Each step is a single pane — no scrolling walls of text
- Lore-appropriate flavor text for each option (keep it terse, evocative, WH40K tone)
- CRT phosphor green aesthetic throughout
- Summary screen at end showing full character before confirming
