# Spawn dungeon enemies in coherent groups instead of only solo contacts

Change hostile generation so environment-appropriate enemies can appear in groups, packs, or squads rather than always as isolated single units. Examples: chaos cultists often in cells of 1-10, vermin in swarms, enforcers in patrol pairs, servitors in work gangs. The generation system should support per-archetype group size rules, room-aware placement, and sensible caps so clusters feel intentional instead of random scatter. Keep the design extensible because later themed-room generation will need to place coordinated encounter sets, not just individual actors.
