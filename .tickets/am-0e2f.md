# Scale monster difficulty and item quality by depth

Feature: as the player progresses deeper into an environment, monsters should become more dangerous and item quality should improve. Depth progression should affect challenge and rewards together so deeper exploration feels meaningfully riskier and more valuable. This work is blocked by am-ifnp because item progression depends on usable item support being in place.
